HOW TO REACH PAJU PROVENCE VILLAGE

Contact for group visit and other inquiries +90 31 946 6353

79-24, Saeori-ro, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do, Republic of Korea
[Land number-based address] 80-10, Seongdong-lee, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do, Republic of Korea

Google Map Google Map
  • CAR
    Punch in the address "69, Saeori-ro, Tanhyeon-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do" into a navigation app and follow its direction
  • SUBWAY
    Take subway and get off at Geumchon Station in Paju and then take a taxi. The ride would take about 20 minutes. (It would cost about 10,000Won)
  • BUS
    Take red long-distance bus No. 2200 near the Exit No. 1 of the Hapjeong subway station, and take off at the Seongdong Intersection bus station. The village is within 10 to 15 minutes of walking distance.
