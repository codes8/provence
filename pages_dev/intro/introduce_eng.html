ABOUT PAJU PROVENCE VILLAGE

Be a true heroine of your life in Paju Provence Village.

Sunlight spills over low hills under a crystal-clear blue sky; a river flows leisurely in floral scents under streams of starlight.
Paju Provence Village is filled with the South Provence's pastoral vibe and culture.
It is a pretty village with a landscape from South France; cozy alleyways; dream-like flower fields and gardens; aroma from freshly baked breads, and; fragrances of the nature's diverse herbs. Paju Provence Village is rich with photo zones with fairy-tale sceneries and spaces for cultural experiences, as well as long-beloved cuisine restaurants.
